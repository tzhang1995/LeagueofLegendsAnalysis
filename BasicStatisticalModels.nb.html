So for every point of vision score, the log odds of winning increases by 0.0096 and at a vision score of ~23.4, your win chance is 50%. But what if we wanted to include role (adc, support, etc.)? Is it more important for a particular role to have a high vision score? It should be noted that the average and standard deviation of vision score is 23.49 and 19.96 respectively, so the large vision scores above 100 are very rare and the win rate should not be taken as accurate.
